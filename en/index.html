THE COMPETITION

Miss Star International is more than a pageant—it's a multidimensional experience that pushes boundaries and embraces the future. Our contestants showcase their talents across innovative challenges designed to identify true global ambassadors.

Phase 1

National Selections

Each franchise conducts local competitions to select their national representative, embodying beauty, intelligence, and purpose.

Phase 2

Virtual Preliminaries

Contestants participate in virtual challenges and interviews, demonstrating their adaptability and technological fluency.

Phase 3

Regional Competitions

Continental showdowns featuring immersive performances and technological innovations unique to each region.

Phase 4

International Finals

The ultimate showcase in Singapore where our finalists compete in futuristic challenges and present their social impact platforms.

Futuristic Wear

Contestants showcase clothing that blends high fashion with technology and sustainability.

Immersive Talent

Performances enhanced with AR/VR technologies creating multi-sensory experiences.

Impact Platform

Innovative solutions to global challenges presented through interactive displays.

AI Interview

Dynamic questions generated by AI to test knowledge, adaptability, and critical thinking.
