Empowering Trans Beauty and Leadership in Peru

National Director

Kayra Flores

Kayra Flores, a prominent figure in Peru's LGBTQ+ activism, has been leading MSI Peru since 2022. With over 15 years of experience in event management and human rights advocacy, she has transformed the franchise into a platform for empowerment and social change.

Under her leadership, MSI Peru has established partnerships with major LGBTQ+ organizations and created opportunities for trans women in various professional fields.

Current Miss Star Peru

Isabella has been working tirelessly to promote trans visibility and rights across Peru. Her platform focuses on education access and workplace inclusion for trans individuals.

Trans Rights in Peru

  • Gender Identity Law (2023): Allows legal gender recognition through administrative process
  • Anti-discrimination Laws: Protection in employment and public services
  • Healthcare Access: Specialized clinics in major cities
  • Education Rights: Non-discrimination policies in educational institutions
  • Workplace Inclusion: Growing corporate initiatives for trans employment
