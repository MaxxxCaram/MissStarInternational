National Director

Kayra Flores

National Director of Miss Star Peru, Kayra Flores brings her extensive experience in pageantry and trans advocacy to lead our Peruvian franchise. Under her direction, Miss Star Peru has become a platform for empowerment and visibility for the trans community in Peru.

Contact: peru@missstarinternational.com

Miss Star Peru 2024

Valentina Mariñas, our reigning Miss Star Peru 2024, represents the beauty, intelligence, and determination of trans women in Peru. Through her platform, she advocates for trans rights and visibility while preparing to represent Peru at Miss Star International 2024.
