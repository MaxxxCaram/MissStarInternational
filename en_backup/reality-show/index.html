About the Show

Miss Star International: The Reality Show takes you behind the scenes of one of the world's most prestigious pageants. Follow the journey of contestants from around the globe as they compete for the crown, navigate challenges, form friendships, and undergo personal transformations.

Our reality show offers an intimate look at the dedication, passion, and hard work that goes into preparing for the international stage. From intense training sessions to emotional breakthroughs, you'll witness every moment of this life-changing experience.

Latest Season

Season 3 of Miss Star International: The Reality Show follows 20 contestants from different countries as they live together, compete in challenges, and prepare for the grand finale. This season introduces new elements, including community service projects, cultural exchange activities, and innovative talent showcases.
