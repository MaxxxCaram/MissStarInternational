National Director

Ava Simoes

Ava Simoes, Miss Star International 2019, has transformed MSI Brazil into a powerful platform for trans visibility and empowerment. As National Director since 2020, she brings her firsthand experience as a former winner to guide and mentor contestants.

Her leadership has established Brazil as a powerhouse in international pageantry, focusing on both inner beauty and social advocacy.

Current Miss Star Brazil

Gabriela Santos

Gabriela has dedicated her reign to promoting education and professional development opportunities for trans youth. Her work with national organizations has created new pathways for trans individuals in the corporate world.

Trans Rights in Brazil

  • Legal Gender Recognition: Available through administrative process since 2018
  • Healthcare Coverage: Gender-affirming procedures covered by SUS (public health system)
  • Anti-discrimination Laws: Federal protection against discrimination based on gender identity
  • Education Access: Growing number of universities with inclusive policies
  • Name Change Rights: Simplified process for legal name and gender marker changes
