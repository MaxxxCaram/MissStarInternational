Our Legacy

The Miss Star International Hall of Fame honors the exceptional individuals who have worn the crown and used their platform to create positive change in the world. Each winner represents not only beauty and talent but also dedication to advocacy, leadership, and making a difference in their communities and beyond.

Our Hall of Fame celebrates the lasting legacy of these remarkable individuals and their continued impact long after their reign has ended. They remain ambassadors of our values and vision, inspiring future generations to pursue excellence and purpose.

2023

Tifanny Colleman

Tiffany's reign focused on environmental conservation and sustainability. She launched the "Green Future" initiative, partnering with organizations to plant over 50,000 trees worldwide.

2022

Veso Golden

Veso dedicated her platform to mental health awareness, creating safe spaces for discussions and advocating for improved access to mental health resources globally.

2021

Mei Lin

Mei Lin championed education equality, establishing scholarship programs for underprivileged students and promoting cultural exchange through her "Bridge of Knowledge" program.

2020

Biw Kanitnum

Biw focused on women's empowerment, creating mentorship programs and vocational training initiatives for women in developing countries.

2019

Ava Simoes

Ava's platform centered on LGBTQ+ rights and inclusion. Her advocacy work led to policy changes and increased awareness about transgender issues globally.

Our Champions

Meet the extraordinary individuals who have earned the title of Miss Star International. Each winner brings their unique story, platform, and vision to the role, leaving an indelible mark on our organization and the world.

2015

Vanessa Lopes

Chile

My crown is a responsibility to be a voice for those who cannot speak and to create positive change in our world.

Vanessa's environmental conservation platform has inspired global initiatives and partnerships with major organizations. Her "Green Future" project continues to grow, with sustainable development programs in over 20 countries.

2022

Rafaella Manfrini

Brazil

Mental health is not a luxury but a necessity. My mission is to break the stigma and create a world where everyone feels safe to seek help.

Rafaella's mental health advocacy has led to partnerships with global health organizations and the establishment of support networks in underserved communities. Her "Mind Matters" initiative provides resources and education worldwide.

2019

Kulchaya Tansiri

Thailand

Education is the most powerful tool we have to change the world. Every child deserves access to quality learning opportunities.

Kulchaya Tansiri's education equality platform has provided scholarships to over 500 students and established learning centers in rural communities. Her cultural exchange programs continue to foster global understanding and cooperation.
