National Director

Polo Hernandez

Polo Hernandez, a renowned figure in Mexico's LGBTQ+ community, brings over two decades of experience in pageantry and human rights activism. As the National Director of MSI Mexico since 2021, they have revolutionized the pageant industry with their innovative approach and commitment to trans representation.

Their vision has established MSI Mexico as a leading platform for trans visibility and empowerment in Latin America.

Trans Rights in Mexico

  • Gender Identity Recognition: Legal in multiple states including Mexico City, Jalisco, and Oaxaca
  • Federal Anti-discrimination Protection: Includes gender identity and expression
  • Healthcare Access: Growing number of specialized clinics and services
  • Education: Inclusive policies in major universities
  • Employment Protection: Several states have explicit protections for trans workers
