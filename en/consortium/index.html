Our Franchises and National Directors

Global Network of Leaders

The Miss Star International Consortium is a prestigious global network of leaders in the beauty and entertainment industry. Our franchises and national directors represent the pinnacle of excellence in pageantry, bringing together diverse talents and perspectives from around the world.

Each franchise holder is carefully selected based on their commitment to our values of diversity, empowerment, and excellence. They play a crucial role in identifying and nurturing talent, organizing national competitions, and representing their countries on the international stage.

Key Benefits for Franchise Holders:

  • Exclusive National License: Sole rights to organize Miss Star International national competitions in their territory.
  • International Development Platform: Access to global resources, training, and networking opportunities.
  • Access to Global Events: Participation in international events, galas, and the annual Miss Star International Grand Finale.
  • Brand Association: Connection with a prestigious international brand that stands for beauty with purpose.
  • Business Opportunities: Potential for partnerships, sponsorships, and media collaborations.
