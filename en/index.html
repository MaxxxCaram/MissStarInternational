Reality Show Project

Miss Star International is evolving. As part of our core strategy to combat global transphobia and respond to the escalating aggression from worldwide right-wing movements, we are progressively transforming our traditional pageant format into an innovative reality show. This transformation represents more than just a change in format - it's a powerful platform for visibility, education, and social change.

The Reality Show takes you behind the scenes, revealing the authentic journeys, challenges, and triumphs of our contestants. By sharing their personal stories, dreams, and daily experiences, we aim to break down prejudices, challenge misconceptions, and showcase the incredible strength, talent, and humanity of our trans community.

This groundbreaking project will:

  • Showcase authentic trans narratives and experiences
  • Challenge stereotypes through personal storytelling
  • Document the transformative journey of contestants
  • Create meaningful dialogue about trans rights and inclusion
  • Build bridges of understanding across communities
  • Empower trans voices in mainstream media
