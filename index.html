About Miss Star International

Miss Star International is more than just a beauty pageant. It's a platform that celebrates trans beauty, talent, and advocacy while creating meaningful change in society.

Our mission is to provide visibility and opportunities for the trans community while challenging stereotypes and promoting inclusivity.
