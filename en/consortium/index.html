About the Consortium

The Miss Star International Consortium represents a global network of franchise directors, each dedicated to discovering and nurturing the next generation of beauty queens who will shape the future of pageantry.

Our Mission

To create a unified platform that celebrates diversity, promotes cultural exchange, and empowers women through innovative pageantry experiences.

  • Foster international collaboration and cultural understanding
  • Implement cutting-edge technologies in pageant production
  • Support charitable initiatives and social causes
  • Develop future leaders in the beauty and entertainment industry
