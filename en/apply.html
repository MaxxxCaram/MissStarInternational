FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Miss Star International?

Miss Star International is a next-generation beauty pageant that combines traditional elements with futuristic technologies and a focus on leadership, innovation, and social impact. We are redefining beauty pageants for the modern era.

How is this different from other pageants?

Unlike traditional pageants, Miss Star International incorporates cutting-edge technology, virtual and augmented reality experiences, and focuses on how contestants can use innovation to drive positive social change. Our platform emphasizes both inner qualities and outer beauty.

What are the key dates for the competition?

The application deadline is June 30, 2025. Virtual preliminaries will take place in August 2025, with regional selections in September. The international finals will be held in November 2025 in Singapore. All dates are subject to change, and participants will be notified.

How are contestants evaluated?

Contestants are evaluated based on multiple criteria: personal presentation, communication skills, leadership qualities, social impact platform, talent showcase, intelligence, and how they embody our core values of innovation, sustainability, and global citizenship.

What is the prize package?

The winner receives the Miss Star International title, a $100,000 scholarship, a global ambassadorship role, a luxury technology package, media exposure, international travel opportunities, and funding to develop their social impact platform.

How can I represent my country if there is no franchise?

If your country does not currently have a Miss Star International franchise, you can apply directly through the international application. Based on the applications received, we may establish a new franchise in your country or select representatives through our global talent search.
